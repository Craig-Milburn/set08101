Welcome

This game has been created as an interactive way of forming your first mind palace. You will work your way through the game making decisions navigate to the endgame. Win or lose you will find an end. Along the way you will find items which will be in bold. The goal is to attempt to remember the items in relation to the rooms that you enter. Map out the rooms in your head, this will help in retrieval of these items.

For more information on mind palaces and other areas of the game there is links in the About section of this site. If you wish to contact me for further information than that found on the site please feel free to use the information on the Contact page.
